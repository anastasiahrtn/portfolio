BEEware: Campus-wide Sexual Harassment Reporting Platform

January 2026

With the increasing awareness of sexual harassment on college campuses, there is a growing need for a comprehensive reporting platform that can provide support and resources to survivors. As a means to address this issue, along with my team, I developed BEEware, a mobile app prototype for a campus-wide sexual harassment reporting platform. The app was designed to provide a safe and confidential space for survivors to report incidents of sexual harassment, access resources and support, and connect with other survivors.

BEEware is packed with multiple features to ensure that survivors have access to the support and resources they need. The app includes a reporting feature that allows survivors to report incidents of sexual harassment in a safe and confidential manner. The app also provides access to resources such as counseling services. BEEware also includes a community feature that allows students to connect with other survivors and share their experiences.

BEEware was developed using PHP, HTML, and CSS, technologies I learned during my time as a full-stack web developer intern. The app was designed with user experience in mind, with a simple and intuitive interface that allows survivors to easily navigate the app and access the resources they need. The development of BEEware was a collaborative effort, with my team working together to design and develop the app, as well as conduct user testing to ensure that the app met the needs of survivors.

The app was also made to match BINUS University's application themes, with a color scheme and design elements that reflect the university's identity. The development of BEEware was a rewarding experience, as it allowed me to apply my skills in web development to create a platform that can make a positive impact on the lives of survivors of sexual harassment on college campuses.
